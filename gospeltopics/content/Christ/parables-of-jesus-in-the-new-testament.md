# Parables of Jesus in the New Testament

Based upon 2019 Reading

Plan — document the parables.  Then, apply the observation of Elder Holland and identify the deeper meaning.  As Elder Holland says, [of the parable of the Laborers in the Vineyard], this parable, like all parables is not really about laborers or wages any more than the others are about sheep and goats.  This is a story about God’s goodness, His patience and forgiveness, and the Atonement of the Lord Jesus Christ.  It is a story about generosity and compassion.  It is a story about grace. 

## Additional Thoughts on Parables:

Parable of the virgins is about preparation. Five were prepared ad received blessings. 

Parable about talents is about putting our talents into worldly things - that can't be multiplied.  How service is employed. 

Parable of the sheep and goats is about direction and being engaged. Using our  preparation and talents to better further the gospel.
