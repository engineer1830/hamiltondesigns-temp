# Anger

Are there different types of anger?

Alma 46: 11 — Moroni was angry with Amalickiah  
Alma 48:7 — Moroni prepared their minds to be faithful unto the Lord

Moroni’s anger resulted because of his love of his people . . . he sought the welfare of his people. Moroni’s anger stirred up his people to righteousness.

Moroni used his anger to prepare his people and turn them to Christ. He had principles and followed doctrine.

What did Moroni’s anger accomplish? He prayed mightily unto his God (vs 13) and (vs 16) he prayed that the cause of the Christians might be favored and (vs 17) poured out his whole soul so that (vs 18) we are not trodden down and destroyed.

Alma 46: 4-10 — Amalickiah seeks his own glory . . .  
Alma 47: 1 — Stirred up his people to anger to bring the Nephites into bondage or kill them outright

Amalickiah used anger to incite and seek revenge
