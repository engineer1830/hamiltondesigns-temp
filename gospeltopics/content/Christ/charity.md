# Charity

Definitions of charity - the pure love of Christ . . . to what end?  Moses 1:39 (bring to pass the immortality and eternal life of man

Another way to characterize charity - Doing that which is required to save the children of our Heavenly Father

What were the actions of Jehovah and Jesus (Old Testament and New Testament Savior)?  Just because we don’t have the full context of everything only means we don’t fully understand the why of things that happened (Acts 5 for example).  We must develop faith that Jesus Christ’s actions are always full of charity, even when we don’t understand the full context.

D&C 98: 40 - Thou shalt forgive as oft as your enemy repents 70x7 . . . Jesse’s commentary . . . if he continues to do the same thing over and over, that is not true repentance and it engages the next passage

D&C 98: 41-44 - If your enemy trespasses 3x and repents not, thou shalt forgive, but if he trespasses a 4th time, thou shalt not forgive and shalt bring these testimonies to the Lord

D&C 98: 45 - If your enemy repents not, the Lord shall avenge thee 100 fold

Distinguish between forgiving and reconciliation  . . . forgiving is what we do while reconciliation requires both parties involved to be willing to change.  

Only viewing “turning the other cheek” as the way to deal with those in the world who would take advantage of you is incorrect.  It is our responsibility to set and hold appropriate boundaries that don’t subsidize and enable behavior towards us that offends God.
