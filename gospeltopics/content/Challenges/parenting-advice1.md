# Parenting Advice From the Scriptures

I wonder if [Eli] has fallen into the trap of thinking, “Well, you know, they have their agency.”

But we have to understand that agency requires consequences too... and if parents if don’t allow children to experience the consequences of their bad choices they've impinged upon their agency just as much as if they’d never given them a choice in the first place.

Professor Terry Ball  
BYU
