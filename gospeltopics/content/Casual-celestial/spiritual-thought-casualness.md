# Spiritual Thought - Casualness

(11/12/23 EQ Presidency)

The word casual has been on my mind often of recent. In my talk a few weeks ago, I made the remark that we cannot be casual and celestial. This happened because I had prepared the talk, but as I got up, I felt that I should not read it (except quotes) and speak to the points that I had written as I had reflected on the topic of A Mighty Change of Heart. In doing so, a comment that I made has stuck with me because it was not mine alone, but a phrase that was given to me — We cannot be casual and celestial. I find that the word casual shows up and is an indication that we’re about to be taught something of value.

President Nelson taught in Spiritual Treasures in 2019:

So, I invite you to study prayerfully section 25 of the Doctrine and Covenants and discover what the Holy Ghost will teach you. Your personal spiritual endeavor will bring you joy as you gain, understand, and use the power with which you have been endowed.
