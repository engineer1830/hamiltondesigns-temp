# Doctrines Restored by Joseph Smith

The Restoration brought back truths that had been lost, fragmented, or misunderstood for centuries.  
These doctrines form the foundation of the restored gospel, and they are inseparable from the prophetic calling of Joseph Smith.

## Core Doctrines Restored

- **Everything we believe about Jesus Christ** — because we believe Jesus Christ personally spoke to Joseph Smith.
- **The power and authority to translate the Book of Mormon**, receive modern revelation, and reshape 2,000 years of Christian understanding.
- **A revealed understanding of who we are and why we are here.**
- **The reality of a premortal life**, where we lived with God before coming to earth.
- **A glorious post‑mortal destiny**, including resurrection and eternal life with our Heavenly Father.
- **The universality of the gospel** — that *all* of God’s children will have a fair and equal opportunity to receive exaltation.
- **A restored understanding of the nature of God.**
- **Eternal marriage and eternal families**, including covenants that continue beyond death.
- **Truths revealed in the Book of Mormon and Doctrine and Covenants**, expanding and clarifying the Bible.
- **The separate and distinct members of the Godhead.**
- **God’s purpose for creating the world** — *“to bring to pass the immortality and eternal life of man”* (Moses 1:39).
- **The doctrine of sealings**, including how eternal relationships are formed and endure in the eternities.
