# August Learnings

## Themes from August Trip with Jenny

The world has reached a point where it increasingly misunderstands or undervalues religion. In order to spiritually survive, we must recognize that The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints is essential because it contains the covenants and saving ordinances required to return to our Heavenly Father. There is no middle ground, and church is not simply about being good or having a community. There is a distinct difference between being “spiritual” and being **active in the Church**, making covenants, and keeping them.
