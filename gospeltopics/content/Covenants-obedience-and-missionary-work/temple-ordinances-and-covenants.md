# Temple ordinances and covenants

Temple ordinances and covenants

Obedience begins before we arrive at the temple:

Ezra 7:10 — For Ezra had prepared his heart to seek the law of the Lord, and to do it, and to teach in Israel statutes and judgments.

How do we prepare?

The nature of the work is described in Nehemiah 6:3 — And I sent messengers unto them, saying, I am doing a great work, so that I cannot come down: why should the work cease, whilst I leave it, and come down to you?

Know where you are and what you’re doing. 

All ordinances are related . . . they all point to Christ . . . from Baptism to Sealing to Spouse
