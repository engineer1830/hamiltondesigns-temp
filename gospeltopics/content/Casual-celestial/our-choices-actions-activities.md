# Our Choices and Actions and Activities
### Does it satisfy or does it sanctify?

Our daily choices reveal what we truly value. Some actions merely **satisfy** — they entertain, distract, or fill time. Other actions **sanctify** — they shape us, refine us, and draw us closer to the Savior.

The difference is rarely about the activity itself. It’s about:

- the **intent** behind it  
- the **spirit** we bring to it  
- the **direction** it points our heart  

A life of discipleship is built from thousands of small, quiet decisions that lean toward sanctification rather than self‑satisfaction. When we choose activities that lift, invite the Spirit, and align our will with God’s, we begin to feel the slow, steady transformation He promises.

The question becomes a simple spiritual compass:

**Does this satisfy me for a moment, or does it sanctify me for eternity?**
