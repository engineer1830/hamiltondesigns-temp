## Enduring to the End

Elder Renlund taught:

“So how can individuals live ‘happily ever after’? ‘The road to eternal happiness depends on lifelong conversion to the truths of the restored gospel of Jesus Christ. Lifelong conversion means that we endure to the end, remaining firm in our commitment to keep the covenants we have made with God — no matter what.’”
