# Thoughts on the Crucifixion

From Jenny

* With the taunting temptations of the Jews when He was on the cross (Matthew 27:11-49)
* The Jews in Jerusalem crucified Christ because of their priestcraft and iniquities
    * Priestcraft = (2 Nephi 26:29) “Men preach and set themselves up for a light unto the world, that they may get gain and praise of the world”
* Matthew 27:18 — For the envy (the priests envied the true light that Christ had . . . priestcraft)
* The Jews tempted and taunted Christ with the same temptations at the end of His life that Satan tempted at the beginning of His ministry (Matthew 4:6)
* The Jews requested the crucifixion and they demanded it and accepted His blood on their hands and their children
* How much power came from His perfect knowledge . . . and the equal opposition and trials to accompany the knowledge and He acted upon His perfect knowledge . . . Total Light experiences Total Darkness
* What is the parallel for us?  What does Satan attack?  He questions our identify because if he can strip you of your divine nature (or your awareness of it), then it is eat, drink and be merry.
