# Make Social Media Positive

You make social media by where you shine the light.

* If you are posting to brag and bring attention to yourself, it is self serving and you have your reward (like the one who stands on the corner and announces that he is fasting)
    * The so named selfie is self explanatory I hope
* If you shine light on the Gospel and on the Savior and to serve, you make social media a positive influence
* Are you inviting all to come unto Christ through your social media utilization

Let your light so shine that you can glorify your Father who is in Heaven
