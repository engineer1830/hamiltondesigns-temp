# Doctrines Restored by Joseph Smith

What doctrines were restored by Joseph Smith and these must be given up by all who reject Joseph Smith and The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints:

* Everything we believe about Jesus Christ because we believe that Jesus spoke to Joseph Smith
* That he received power to translate the Book of Mormon and receive the revelations we have and transform 2000 years of Christianity
* Our understanding of who we are and why we are here
* That we had a pre-mortal life
* That we will have a glorious post-mortal life with our Father again after this life
* The gospel is for ALL and they’ll have an opportunity to be exalted - but w/o Joseph, there is not an equal chance at eternity and salvation
* What we believe about God
* Eternal marriage and families and marriages in heaven
* All the truths from the Book of Mormon and Doctrine and Covenants
* Separate members of the Godhead
* Why God created the world (Moses 1:39) - glorifies Himself by exalting ALL of his children
* How sealings work in eternity
